What We Repair

Commercial Refrigeration Equipment We Fix

If it keeps your stock cold and your business compliant, we service it. Here's what we repair most for Sydney commercial clients.

Display & Glass-Door Fridges

Front-of-house display fridges losing their cool? We repair compressor, fan and seal faults fast so stock stays safe and presentable.

Bar & Beverage Coolers

Warm beer taps and bottle fridges cost you sales. We get bar and beverage coolers back to temperature without disrupting service.

Ice Machines

Commercial ice machines running slow, leaking, or not producing ice. Full diagnostics and repair for hospitality and retail sites.

Food Prep & Undercounter Units

Prep bench and undercounter fridge faults affecting food safety. Fast repairs to keep your kitchen compliant and running.

Blast Chillers

Blast chiller not hitting target temperature? We diagnose refrigerant, control and airflow faults on commercial units.

Walk-In Coolers & Freezers

Walk-in units with door, seal, compressor or temperature problems. We handle structural and refrigeration faults on-site.

Supermarket Display Cabinets

Multi-deck and island display cabinets need specialist attention. We service supermarket and grocery refrigeration equipment.

Cool Rooms & Refrigerated Storage

Cool room running warm or icing up? We diagnose and repair refrigeration faults across all commercial storage sites.
